Baked Asian Meatballs

This is a great easy meal or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground turkey (or your favorite meat)
  • 3/4 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 whole green onion, chopped (or a 2 cubes of onion powder if you prefer)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon low sodium soy sauce

Asian Sauce

  • 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
  • 1/8 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • sesame seeds for serving

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F or 204 C
  2. Mix Asian sauce ingredients in a separate bowl and set aside
  3. Add meat, breadcrumbs, garlic, green onions, egg, sesame oil, and soy sauce to mixing bowl
  4. Mix with hands until combined
  5. Grease a baking sheet with oil and portion out 12 meatballs (wet hands helps)
  6. Place meatballs on the oiled baking sheet and cook for 15 minutes or until fully cooked, flipping halfway through
  7. Take out of the oven and toss with Asian sauce
  8. Serve immediately and garnish if desired
