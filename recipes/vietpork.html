Vietnamese Caramelized Pork

Vietnamese caramelized pork is my favorite dish as of recently, and I believe you'll enjoy it too!

Ingredients

  • 400 grams of minced pork (or whatever minced meat you prefer)
  • 1 tablespoon of minced ginger
  • 1.25 tablespoons of fish sauce
  • 3.5 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 3 chili peppers (ceyenne or thai)
  • 1 cube of onion power or a teaspoon of diced onion
  • 2 tablespoons of cooking oil

Steps

  1. Dice the chili peppers
  2. Mince the ginger
  3. Add the cooking oil to a medium sized frying pan on low-medium heat
  4. Add the garlic, peppers, ginger, and onion powder to the oil and saute
  5. Add the meat to the pan
